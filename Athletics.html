Athletics

Athletics is a group of sporting events that involve running, jumping, and throwing. Some of the most common events include sprints, long-distance races, high jump, long jump, and javelin throw. Athletics is a major part of the Olympic Games and tests the strength, speed, and endurance of athletes.
